Government of Abia State

Executive, Legislative, and Judicial Structure serving the people.

His Excellency, Dr. Alex Otti

Dr. Alex Chioma Otti, OFR is the Governor of Abia State, Nigeria, sworn in on 29 May 2023 after winning the 2023 election on the Labour Party platform. Born on 18 February 1965, he hails from Isiala-Ngwa South LGA, Abia State. He holds a First Class degree in Economics from the University of Port Harcourt and an MBA from the University of Lagos, with executive training from top global business schools. Dr. Otti is a renowned banker and economist, having served as Group Managing Director/CEO of Diamond Bank Plc before retiring in 2014. He is also a writer and former newspaper columnist. As governor, he focuses on infrastructure, economic reform, healthcare, and workers’ welfare. He is married to Priscilla Otti and they have three children.

Executive

Led by the Executive Governor, this arm implements laws and oversees the daily administration of the state. It includes the Deputy Governor, Secretary into State Government, and Commissioners of various Ministries.

Legislature

The Abia State House of Assembly is the legislative arm responsible for making laws for the peace, order, and good government of the state. It also performs oversight functions.

Judiciary

The independent arm that interprets the laws. The state judiciary comprises the High Court, Customary Court of Appeal, and Magistrates' Courts, ensuring justice and the rule of law.

Local Government Areas

Abia State consists of 17 Local Government Areas (LGAs) that bring government closer to the grassroots. Each LGA is headed by a Chairman and Councilors.
